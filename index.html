David Davidson

Engaging, Professional, Male Voice Over Artist

For as long as I can remember, I've always loved using my voice to entertain, inform, and connect with people. Encouraged by friends, colleagues, and even strangers who recognized my natural talent for vocal delivery, I decided to take the next step and turn a longtime personal passion into a profession.

Today, I'm a professional voiceover artist with a well-defined tone and a wide vocal range. Whether you're looking for a hard-sell commercial, an engaging educational narration, or a warm, friendly voice for your business messaging, I bring flair, sophistication, and professionalism to every project. My experience spans everything from audio and video narration to character voices, promo tags, station IDs, commercials, live radio, PBX and voicemail systems, on-hold messaging and much much more!

Based in Northern California, I'm dedicated, reliable, and easy to work with. Most importantly, I truly enjoy what I do, and I believe that joy comes through in every performance. Take a moment and listen to my
